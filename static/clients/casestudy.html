Olympic Case Study

Background: As soon as the 2021 Olympics ended in Tokyo, The International Olympic Committee begin searching for the location of the 2025 Summer Olympic Games. It is the members of the IOC, meeting at their Session, who choose the host. Electing the host for the Olympic Games is one of the powers of the Session. The host city is elected by a majority of the votes cast by secret ballot. We are honored that the IOC has decided to work with our consulting company IRA Story Tellers, Inc. in order to create visualizations based on previous Olympic Games, that will be used in the decision making process.

The Olympic committee has requested that we include

  • Data visualizations analyzing past Olympic events.
